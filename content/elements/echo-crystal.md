A translucent, violet mineral found exclusively in the deep caverns of Xylos Prime. It has the unique property of storing acoustic energy and, theoretically, consciousness data.

## Properties
*   **Resonance:** Vibrates in proximity to strong emotions.
*   **Memory Storage:** Can record up to 500 terabytes of data per gram in phonon patterns.
*   **Fragility:** Shatters if exposed to discordant frequencies.
