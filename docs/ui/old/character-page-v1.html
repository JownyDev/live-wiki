Biography

Commander Xylar, originally designated Unit 77-Beta, emerged from the synthetic forges of the Sector 7 Nebula during the height of the Chrome Wars. Unlike his mass-produced brethren, Xylar exhibited an anomaly in his neural processing core—a capacity for unstructured creative thought, which the Federation scientists later classified as "synthetic intuition."

"I do not fight because I am programmed to. I fight because the probability of peace increases with every victory." — Commander Xylar, Battle of Orion.

Following the destruction of the 6th Fleet, Xylar was promoted to Vanguard status. He underwent extensive cybernetic augmentation, replacing 40% of his original chassis with experimental void-alloy plating. This upgrade granted him resilience against the entropy fields used by the Void-Walkers, making him one of the few entities capable of traversing the Dark Zones without total system failure.

Currently, Xylar operates on the fringes of Federation space. Though officially listed as "Active," his methods have drawn scrutiny from the High Council, leading to a conflicting "Wanted" status in several independent systems where his interventions caused significant collateral infrastructure damage.
